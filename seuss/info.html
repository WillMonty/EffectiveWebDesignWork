About Seuss-A-Palooza
  • When? February 12th, Noon
  • Where? New York Public Library (New York, New York)
    • Directions
  • What? An event to celebrate and honor the literary works of Dr. Seuss.
    • Family friendly. Dr. Seuss character costumes are encouraged!
    • All sessions are listed below.
Time Session Description
12:00 PM Doors & Lunch Families can sit down for a meal at Amy's Bread Café.
1:00 PM Opening Ceremony The children will line up at the entrance and parade through the library
to show their Dr. Seuss costumes and see all of the book sections.
2:00 PM Dr. Seuss Readings At the Children's Center, costumed volunteers will be reading some of our most favorite Seuss books to the children.
3:00 PM Seuss Safari Children will be given instructions to find different Seuss book locations with stamps throughout the library.
Those who collect all the stamps will get free Seuss sticker sets!
